Guidance

To start

Before the experiment, it's highly recommended to use Chrome web browser to view this page. Using IE will bring unexpected results.

In this experiment, you need to complete all the four games as below. You can test the games in the Games(Test Mode) with unlimited chances. In the formal submit-mode, only 3/5 chances are provided for your team. It is noticed that you and your partners share the limited trial chances!

You can get back by clicking Menu any time.

Games(Test Mode)

In the test-mode, problem set is much easier than the submit-mode. So, to practise your algorithm/policy is very important here. After fully testing, you and your partners can be ready to finish a submit-mode(bottles are more).

  • Adaptive Game 1
    • Rule: 2 mice, 1 poisoned bottle, you can feed a mouse in 1 step with some bottles. Mouse will die immediately when drinks the poisoned bottle.
    Target: Use the least steps.
    Introduction:

    Try to find the only poisoned bottle with the least steps. If your strategy covers all the situations, you win , otherwise, you lose. The best result will be counted.
    You and your partners will have 3 chances to submit totally.

  • Adaptive Game 2
    • Rule: 2 mice, 1 poisoned bottle, you can feed a mouse in 1 step with some bottles. Mouse will die immediately when drinks the poisoned bottle(counted as 1 step).
    Target: Use the least steps.
    Introduction:

    Try to find the only poisoned bottle with the least steps. If your strategy covers all the situations, you win , otherwise, you lose. The average results will be counted.
    You and your partners will have 5 chances to submit totally.

  • Non-Adaptive Game
    • Rule: 1 poisoned bottle, limited to 1 step to find out the poisoned bottle.
    Target: Use the least mice.
    Introduction:

    Try to find the only poisoned bottle with the least mice. Note that you must arrange the bottles to each mouse, then submit the result. If your strategy covers all the situations, you win , otherwise, you lose. Best result will be counted.
    You and your partners will have 3 chances to submit totally.

  • Hybrid-Adaptive Game
    • Rule: 2 mice, 1 poisoned bottle, poisoned mouse will die a week later.
    Target: Use the least weeks.
    Introduction:

    Try to find the only poisoned bottle with the least weeks. Arrange the bottles to the mice, then click the "Next week" button and poisoned bottles will effect in the next week. The best result will be counted.
    You and your partners will have 3 chances to submit totally.
