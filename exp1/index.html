Job Rule

In this homework, you need complete all the three games as below. You can test the games in the Games(Test Mode) with unlimited times. In the formal submit-mode, only three times are provided for you, the system will automatically take the highest score as your final score.

Games(Test Mode)

In the test-mode, problem set is much easier than the submit-mode. So, to practise your algorithm/policy is very important here. After fully testing, you can be ready to finish a submit-mode(bottles are more).

  • Adaptive Game 1
    • Rule: 2 mice, 1 poisoned bottle, mouse will die immediately when drinks the poisoned bottle(counted as 1 step).
    Target: use the least steps.
    Introduction:

    Try to find the only poisoned bottle with the least steps. If your strategy covers all the situations, you win , otherwise, you lose. The best results will be counted.

  • Adaptive Game 2
    • Rule: 2 mice, 1 poisoned bottle, mouse will die immediately when drinks the poisoned bottle(counted as 1 step).
    Target: use the least steps.
    Introduction:

    Try to find the only poisoned bottle with the least steps. If your strategy covers all the situations, you win , otherwise, you lose. The average results will be counted.

  • Non-Adaptive Game
    • Rule: 1 poisoned bottle, limited to 1 step to find out the poisoned bottle.
    Target: use the least mice.
    Introduction:

    Try to find the only poisoned bottle with the least mice. Note that you must arrange the bottles to each mouse, then submit the result. If your strategy covers all the situations, you win , otherwise, you lose.

  • Hybrid-Adaptive Game
    • Rule: 2 mice, 1 poisoned bottle, poisoned mouse will die a week later.
    Target: use the least weeks.
    Introduction:

    Try to find the only poisoned bottle with the least weeks. Arrange the bottles to the mice, then click the "Next week" button and poisoned bottles will effect in the next week. Note that you can only have one overlapped bottle of distinct mice.
